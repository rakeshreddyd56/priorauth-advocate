03 / 12The cost
A real fight

Meet Rakesh.

32 · Crohn's disease · Aetna PPO through his employer. His gastroenterologist prescribes Humira, the standard biologic. Aetna sends back a denial.

What just happened

1Dr. Patel writes the prescription.
2Her office faxes a prior-auth request to Aetna.
3Aetna routes it to eviCore, a utilization-management vendor whose internal AI tool insiders call "the dial."
4"The dial" denies the request — azathioprine trial was 8 weeks; policy requires 12.
5Rakesh receives a 4-page letter in CPT codes.
DENIED

The denial does not tell him that 82% of appealed Medicare Advantage denials get overturned. Or that external review exists. Or that he has 180 days.
